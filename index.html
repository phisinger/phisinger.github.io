About Me

I'm an aspiring data analyst from Germany. I'm mostly interested in getting valuable insights from data using the simplest means and not the fanciest algorithm. I want to help others understanding their data and make data-driven decisions.
Most of my data science projects are with Python and associated libraries while I'm deepening my skills at SQL, Tableau and Python.

I've finished my corporate bachelor degree in computer science with focus on data science. At the moment, I'm studying Information Systems Engineering and Management at Karlsruhe Institut for Technology (KIT). I have been working as a Business Architect at Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) for more than five years. My major topics have been data platforms, data strategy and Trustworthy AI.
