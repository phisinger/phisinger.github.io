About Me

I'm an amateur data scientist from Germany. I'm mostly interested in getting valuable insights from data using the simplest means and not the fanciest algorithm. I want to help others understanding their data and make data-driven decisions.
Most of my data science projects are with Python and associated libraries and I'm deepening my skills at SQL, Tableau and Python.

I've just finished my corporate bachelor degree in computer science with focus on data science. At the moment, I'm studying "Information Systems Engineering and Management" at Karlsruhe Institut for Technology (KIT) in Karlsruhe, Germany. I have been working as a corporate student at Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) for more than three years. My major topics have been data architectures, data pipelining and AI.
